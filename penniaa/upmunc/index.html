About the Ivy League Model United Nations Conference

The IAA organizes and hosts the University of Pennsylvania Model United Nations Conference (UPMUNC) annually. UPMUNC, in its forty-eight year, is one the world's preeminent collegiate Model United Nations conferences. Each November, UPMUNC hosts more than 1500 students from all over the world in historic Philadelphia.

This year UPMUNC XLVIII will be held from November 6 to November 9, 2014 at the Sheraton in Downtown Philadelphia.
